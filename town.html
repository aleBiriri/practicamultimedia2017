Description

At the foot of the mountain, Castillo awaits for us with his almost houndred villagers. Attached with the forest, surrounded by little animal and harvest farms, Castillo offers you a unique quiet restful adventure, with lots of tracks and his marvelous and middle tempered pool.

Walk through Las Hurdes

Location

This little village belongs to Pinofranqueado, a bigger village, near Caminomorisco, Montehermoso, or if you the big cities, Plasencia or Coria. From Pinofranqueado starts a wonderful little but well mantained road, that will drive you to La Muela, Avellanar, Robledo and eventually Castillo.
